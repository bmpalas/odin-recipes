Scramble Tofes

How to make Scramble Tofes:

This is a damn good meal! Add some fried potatoes and make a burrito!

Ingredients:

  • tofu
  • oil
  • dijon mustard
  • turmeric
  • kala namak
  1. Heat some oil.
  2. Tear off chunks of the tofu and press the water out with your hands.
  3. Put the tofu in the pan and scramble up and fry at high heat for a while.
  4. Add some dijon mustard and some turmeric for color.
  5. Add some kala namak (himalayan black salt) for sulfurous eggy flavor.
  6. Et voila!
